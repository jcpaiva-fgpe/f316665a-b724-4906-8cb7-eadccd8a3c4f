Great!

In the board game Awesome! each of four players gets dots in six colors: red, green, blue, orange, yellow and purple. O knowledge of the rules of this game is not important for the problem at hand Follow. The relative position of each player at the end of the game is determined by the score in the color with the lowest number of points. Win the player who has the most points in his worst color. When two players have the same score in the worst color, the tie is broken by worst color and so on. For example: be the scores given by the table:
Red Green Blue Orange Yellow Purple
Joana 11 18 8 17 18 14
Peter 8 18 18 9 18 18
Miguel 11 18 18 18 18 18
Beatriz 13 12 18 18 15 18
Beatriz will be the winner (with 12 points) and in second place is the Miguel (with 11 points). Joana and Pedro have the same score in the worst color (8 points), but Joana has the best result at smaller (11 points against 9 from Pedro); Joana is third and Pedro is last place.

Task


Write a program that reads the scores of the players and writes the order relative between these. The scoring table is given: 4 lines; each row has the 6 scores of a player (they are positive integers). The program must write a single line with the relative order among the players, of the best for the worst ranked. There will be no draws. We identify the players by numbers from 1 to 4: player 1 has the scores on the first line, the player 2 the second, etc.

Example

Input 


11 18 8 17 18 14
8 18 18 9 18 18
11 18 18 18 18 18
13 12 18 18 15 18

Output 


4 3 1 2
