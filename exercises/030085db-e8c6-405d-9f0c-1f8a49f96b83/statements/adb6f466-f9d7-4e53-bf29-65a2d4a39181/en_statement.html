Ricochet Robots

The Rob√¥s Ricochete, Lda. factory produces autonomous robots for cleaning of large surfaces in post-work hours. The robots move on a grid in the plane executing sequences of the following instructions:

F
move forward until you encounter an obstacle;
D
rotate on itself to the right;
AND
rotate on itself to the left.

The figure illustrates a robot's path on a 9 x 6 grid, starting at the bottom left corner and executing the sequence "F, D, F, E, F, D, F, D, F, D, F ".

It is intended to determine the total distance traveled by the robot (number of visited cells); the size of the grid, the position of the obstacles and sequence of instructions. To simplify the statement, it is assumed that each obstacle occupies exactly one cell of the grid; it is also assumed that the robot is initially in the lower corner left (coordinates (0,0)) facing upwards (i.e. in the direction of the vector (0.1)).

Task

Write a program to determine the total distance traveled by the robot.

The problem data is entered in the following order, a value per each row: the number of columns and the number of rows in the grid followed by the number of obstacles and the coordinates of each obstacle (column, row); finally, the sequence of instructions from the robot (using characters F , D , E ). You can assume that the grid has maximum dimension 30 x 30 and that the robot program has a maximum of 50 instructions.

The output is an integer that represents the total distance traveled by the robot.
