Arrest the usual suspects!

Problem

A well-known multinational software , who prefers remain anonymous, was the victim of a computer attack on the repository of code for the next version of one of your most widespread products. You crackers introduced a series of viruses that were thus first propagated through licensed software !

The attack was so well done that it was not yet possible to determine its origin. It is only known that the authors will have gained access to the network of company using one or more company employee accounts. Comparing the modification dates of the altered files, a time interval in which the attack was carried out.

At the moment all company employees are suspicious, the mood is unsustainable and the investigation of this incident does not proceed. To reduce the number of suspects it was proposed to compare the authentication records of employees. These records indicate the date and time of either the start or at the end of each work session. An employee will be suspicious if a of their work sessions overlap with the period in which the attack. Therefore, a session will be suspect if it starts before the end the attack and finish after the attack begins.

Assignment

Write a program to determine the number of suspects who would be located in this way.

Input

In the first line of the data, there are the initial and final instants of the attack. Then an integer n follows with the number of sessions to be process. The following n lines describe the sessions, ordered time: in each line there is the employee's number, the beginning and end of the respective session.

The company has no more than 100 employees. In the data provided, the moments of the beginning and end of the attack and of the sessions, are negative (representable in 32 bits). The times are all indicated in seconds, corresponding to an interval that has elapsed since a certain initial instant.

Output

The program output is limited to the number of suspects detected (ends by line change)

Input example 


2574 3390
6
10 1760 2330
2 1761 2574
13 2341 2522
10 3377 4885
3 3393 7811
1 4173 4814

Output example 


1
