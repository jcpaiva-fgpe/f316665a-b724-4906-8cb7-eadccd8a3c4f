Meeting

Problem

`` Dans l'Océan Indien, unite petite terre française s'emploie à bousculer les clichés habituels des tropicales. Des cocotiers et des plages, il y en a, bien sûr, à la Réunion. L'eau turquoise du lagon, l'ourlet blanc de la barrière de corail sont au rendez-vous des rêves exotic. ''

They say that on Réunion Island it is not easy to arrange meetings. There are many other chores .... To please some and others one tries to know what is the availability of each. In the end, we expect to be able to set an hour in an interval of mutual agreement, and how, in the middle of the interval, virtue, such will be the time of the meeting.

Task

Make a program that gives a set of intervals , with whole extremes and between 8 and 18, will set as the time of meeting the midpoint of the common interval, or indicate, if impossible .

Input

A number is given of people involved and on the following lines pairs , , separated by space, indicating the people.

Output

The output will be `` impossible '' if there is no time possible or `` x '' or else `` x and a half '', x being an integer.

Example 1: input 


3
10 15
8 12
11 12

Example 1: output 


11:30

Example 2: input 


4
10 15
8 12
11 12
17 19

Example 2: output 


impossible

Example 3: input 


two
10 15
15 16

Example 3: output 


15
