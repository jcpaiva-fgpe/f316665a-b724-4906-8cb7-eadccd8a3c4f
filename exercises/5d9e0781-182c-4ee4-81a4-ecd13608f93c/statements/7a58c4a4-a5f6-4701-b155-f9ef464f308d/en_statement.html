Room!

In the Room game, players alternately place one of 16 pieces on a positions. The pieces have four dichotomous characteristics:

color
white or black;
height
low or high;
shape
round or square;
consistency
hollow or solid.
The first player who places four pieces in a row wins the game horizontal, vertical or diagonal with at least one common feature between them (i.e. four pieces of the same color, of the same height, in the same way or with the same consistency).

Task

It is intended to analyze the description of a board and indicate whether the condition victory was satisfied. 4 lines of text are given corresponding to the 4 board lines; each piece on the board is represented by a 4 letter sequence: P or B (black or white); a or A (low or high); R or Q (round or square); O or S (hollow or solid). The empty positions on the board are represented by 4 dashes - .

The result should be FOUR if the winning condition is satisfied or NOTHING otherwise.
