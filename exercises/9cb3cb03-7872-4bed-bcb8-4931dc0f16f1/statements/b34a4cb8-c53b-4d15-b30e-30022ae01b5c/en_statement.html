Chic Barber Shop


(Photo by Michel Waldmann )

Chic Barber Shop is not big but it is very clean. Three chairs, where the customers wait for their turn before going to the barber's chair, and little more there. The barber does not like having clients standing get in the way. So when there are no vacancies, customers do not enter! But, they can enter if they arrive at the moment when another one is ready, since that Chic is open. Chic opens at 9:00 am and closes at 12:00 pm lunch. Reopens at 15:00 and closes at 19:00. Customers arriving at at 12:00 or until 19:00, inclusive, are served, if Chic is not full. Service is on a first-come, first-served basis. Customers do not wait if the barber is free and do not wait at the door when the Chic is closed or full. There are no hands to measure to not lose customers nor make them wait too long. It is intended to simulate the functioning of Chic on a certain day, assuming that at most one customer will arrive at most and that the barber does not spend more than forty minutes with each client (which always gives you some time for lunch).

Task

Write a program to determine the number of customers who are not were able to enter, and that it will write Lost = r , followed by change line, where r is that number. The program will analyze a ordered sequence of K occurrences H M D , where H and M define the instant the customer arrived ( H hours and M minutes) and D the time (in minutes) that the barber would spend attending the client if he can to do. Thus, 9 2 10 indicates that the customer arrived at 9:02 and the barber would spend 10 minutes if he answered. The value of K is given on the first line.

Example 1

11
9 2 10
9 5 20
9 7 10
9 20 25

9 25 15
16 30 12

18 2 15
18 20 35
19 5 10

Output 


Lost = 2
