Sportsmanship has "its limits". Supporters usually celebrate enthusiastically the goals of your national team, unless they are without a voice or have bought the ticket of an "adept" of the opposing team. Let us analyze the conviction of a Portuguese, if he managed to shout something!

Assignment

Determine how many o 's and / or O ' s have the first sequence of consecutive o's in a shout. If there are neither o 's nor The 's number is zero.

Input

It has the sequence in a line, which will only have letters and a starting point exclamation at the end (maximum 100 letters).

Output

A line with the desired value.

Example

Input 

GaOolooooooooo!

Output 

two
