I am multiple

In the universe of talking numbers, we found one that always crosses with another number, it says whether or not it is a multiple of it.

Task

Write a program to analyze a meeting and determine what the talking number would say.

Input

A line with two positive integers, the second being the speaker. These integers are less than 20000.

Output

A line with the phrase I am a multiple of X or I am not multiple of X , where X will be replaced by the first number given, and the answer will be right.

Example 1

Input 


5 10

Output 


I'm a multiple of 5

Example 2

Input 


7 13

Output 


I'm not a multiple of 7
