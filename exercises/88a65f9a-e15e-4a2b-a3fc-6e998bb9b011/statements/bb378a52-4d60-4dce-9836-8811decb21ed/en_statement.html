Summer Course


Ana and Carlos, who live in Porto, were excited about the idea in July to go to the University of Algarve to attend a summer course for secondary school students. So they are already doing the preparations for the trip.

The ideal is to go by train, from Porto to Faro. The normal price of the trip, in tourist class, it is € 51.50. Fortunately, as both are young people with under the age of 26, are automatically entitled to a 25% discount. Now 25% of 51.50 is 12.87.

In fact, the exact amount would be slightly higher, but the CP makes the discount accounts up to cents. Soon, the price of the trip would be € 38.63. Only that the CP, to facilitate change (they say ...) rounds all prices up, up to the immediate 50 cent multiple. Or that is, the trip costs 39 euros each.

It wouldn't be bad anymore, but Ana and Carlos discovered that if they buy the ticket five days in advance or more will have a discount additional 40%. That is, as 40% of 39 is 15.6, the price with the 40% discount and such rounding to the multiples of 50 cents is at € 23.50. In fact, if the purchase is eight days or more in advance, the discount goes up to 65%, which is even more advantageous.

But the possible discounts don't stop there, Ana and Carlos: if you can bring at least two other friends with you, become a group and are entitled to a discount additional 50%. I mean, if all four (or more) had decided to buy the ticket at the same time, with five days of advance, the discount on 23.50 would be 11.75, so, having made the accounts, and considering the ill-fated rounding, the trip for each one of the group members would stay for € 12.00. Of course if you buy with eight days or more in advance, the price will be even lower.

This discount scheme applies to all long-haul trips. Per so, if Ana and Carlos end up deciding to go to summer school to another site, for example, the Universidade Nova de Lisboa, will have to make similar accounts, starting with the price of the Porto-Lisbon trip.

Of course, instead of doing the math by hand, it is best to write a program that takes care of that.

Task

Write a program that, given the normal price, the advance of purchase and the group size (all young), determine the price of each ticket, expressed by a whole number of cents.

Input

A line with three numbers, representing, respectively, the normal price of the trip (in cents), the advance of the purchase (in days) and the size of the group of travelers, all young.

Restrictions

\ (100 \ leq p <100 \, 000 \) Normal price of the trip (in cents)

Output

A line with a number that represents the price of each ticket, expressed by a whole number of cents (which must be a multiple of 50, because of that rounding rule).

Example 1

Input 

5150 5 4

Output 

1200

Example 2

Input 

3400 10 2

Output 

900
