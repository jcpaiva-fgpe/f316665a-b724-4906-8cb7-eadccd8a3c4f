Problem

where P is the individual's weight in kilograms and A is his height in meters. According to the BMI result, we can classify whether an individual is underweight, adequate weight, overweight or obese. on one adult individuals the reference levels of BMI are:


BMI Step
less than 18.5 Low weight
equal to or greater than 18.5 and less than 25 normal
equal to or greater than 25 and less than 30 pre obesity
equal to or greater than 30 and less than 35 grade I obesity
equal to or greater than 35 and less than 40 grade II obesity
equal to or greater than 40 grade III obesity

Task

Write a program that gives the weight (in grams) and the height (in millimeters) of an individual calculate their BMI step.

Input

The input is given as two integers P (<= 200000) and A (<= 3000) that represent weight (in grams) and height (in millimeters) respectively of an individual.

Output

The output will be a line that shows the BMI step of the individual in cause according to the table above.

Example 1: Input 


63000 1650

Example 1: Output 


normal

Example 2: Input

Example 2: Output
