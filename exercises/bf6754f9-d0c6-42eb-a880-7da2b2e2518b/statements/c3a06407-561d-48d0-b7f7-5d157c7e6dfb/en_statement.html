After all there is still metro

The metro administration decided to replace the automatic ticket sales at the stations, because, being very sophisticated, the the truth is that they are very expensive and break down a lot. The new machines are more cheaper and more robust, but unfortunately they are more basic: they require that buyer enter the exact amount (they do not give change, therefore) and in addition In addition, they do not accept more than four coins at a time, in the same purchase. For example, if the ticket price was € 1.30, the buyer could introduce a one euro coin and a 20 cent coin and a 10 cent coin cents, or one for one euro and three for 10 cents, but the machine would refuse if payment was made with one euro coin, two of 10 cents and two of 5 cents.

Therefore, travel prices have to be chosen in such a way that can be paid with four coins. For example, there can be no trips costing € 3.35, as we would need at least five coins to pay that amount.

Therefore, at the time of increasing the ticket price, the new price has to be chosen in such a way that it can be paid with four coins, when much. The administration does not want to lose money and, therefore, if the new value thought by the administration is not acceptable by the machines, this value will have to be increased, as little as possible, in order to an acceptable value is reached. Returning to the example above, if the management initially wanted the new price to be € 3.35, it would have increase it to € 3.40, which can now be paid in four currencies.

The problem is, given the price the administration wants and the values ​​of the currencies that the machine accepts, calculate the lowest price greater than or equal to the target price that can be paid with four of these currencies. Note that machine is configured so as not to accept all currencies in circulation (for example, you may not accept coins of one cent or two cents, or two euros). Furthermore, it is not impossible for other coins will come into circulation soon (for example, 3 cent coins or 25 cents, as there are in some countries). That’s why it’s necessary also indicate the currency values ​​that the machine is prepared for accept.

Task

Write a program to calculate the ticket price, given the value that administration intends and the currency values ​​that the machine accepts. O price must be the lowest value greater than or equal to the price intended by the administration that can be formed using a maximum of four currencies from those the machine accepts.

The data for the program is entered in the following order: first, the price intended by the administration, in cents; then on the line next, a number, indicating how many types of coins the machine accepts; finally, on the next line, a list of numbers, with so many numbers how many types of currencies, with the value corresponding to each one of these types of currencies, expressed in cents, in ascending order.

The program will write the calculated price for the ticket, in the form of a whole number of cents.

We know that the price sought by the administration is not higher than the quadruple the value of the highest value currency and the number of coins different will never be greater than 10.

Example 1

Input 

335
6
5 10 20 50 100 200

Output 

340

Example 2

Input 

44
5
1 3 10 20 50

Output 

44

Example 3

Input 

90
3
5 10 25

Output 

100
