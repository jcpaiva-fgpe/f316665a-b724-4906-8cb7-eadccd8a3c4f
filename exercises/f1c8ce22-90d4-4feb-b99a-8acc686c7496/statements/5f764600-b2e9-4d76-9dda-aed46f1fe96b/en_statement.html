Chemistry for nothing



When Rui was studying Chemistry, writing formulas of compounds in the notebook (like CaCO 3 , Na 2 CO 3 and H 2 SO 4 ), appeared the younger sister, who stayed fascinated with those sequences of letters and figures. The worst is that she he was not silent, demanding that he explain to him how he divided a sequence big in little pieces. Desperate, Rui enunciated the rules:

  • Each sequence of letters that begins with a large letter (the sister knew the word "capital") represents an element;

  • If there is a number after the element, this is the number of atoms of the element; if not, the number of atoms in the element is 1.

Then, he exemplified with the calcium carbonate formula, CaCO 3 , which has a calcium atom (Ca), a carbon atom (C) and three oxygen atoms (O).

The sister was very happy and wanted to learn more chemistry. But he convinced her to practice. In order to be able to return to the study quickly, decided to write "chemical formulas" for nothing and even invented symbols chemicals with 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 letters. For example, it gave you the formula Ai 19 Xpto 5 Uus (which has size 12) for decompose.

Task

Write a program that, given a chemical formula invented by Rui and ending with ". ", presents the elements that occur in the formula and the number of atoms for each.

Input

A line with a chemical formula (invented by Rui) terminated by ". ". The formula is a sequence of \ (n \) letters and numbers, that starts with a capital letter and does not have a zero immediately after a letter, not even a lower case immediately after a number. No element has more than five letters, nor occurs more than once in the formula. The number of digits followed does not exceed \ (n-1 \).

Restrictions

\ (1 \ leq n \ leq 1 \, 000 \) Formula size

Output

The output has one line for each element that occurs in the formula. Each line has an element, a space, the number of atoms of that element and a line change. Elements must appear in the order in which they occur in the formula.

Example 1

Input 

CaCO3.

Output 

Ca 1
C 1
The 3

Example 2

Input 

Ai19Xpto5Uus.

Output 

Ai 19
Step 5
Uus 1
