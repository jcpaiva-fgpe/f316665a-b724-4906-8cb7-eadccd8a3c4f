When is ToPAS?

We are often asked: "When is ToPAS this year?". It's simple. O ToPAS is usually on the Friday of the academic week of the University Porto, which in turn takes place in the first full week of the month of May. That is, it is the Friday of the first week whose Sunday falls in May. You can even make a program to determine this date!

It is only necessary to know how to calculate the day of the week for a date calendar represented by year , month and day . A simple way to do this is to use the Tondering algorithm and calculate the following expression. 

   (year + year / 4 - year / 100 + year / 400 + Table [month] + day)% 7

The value of the expression is an integer between 0 and 6, inclusive, where 0 interpreted as Sunday, 1 as Monday and so on. Before evaluating it, it is necessary to start by subtracting 1 per year if the month before March (January or February).

In this expression the % operator corresponds to the module operation (remainder of the entire division), with the divisions also indicated as integers. The expression also depends on the month, this function being defined by the table that follows.

Month Value Month Value
January 0 February 3
March two April 5
May 0 June 3
July 5 August 1
September 4 October 6
November two December 4

For the ToPAS date, which is what you want after all, this formula simplifies because May is later than March and the respective value of table is zero.

Task

Write a program that works like a ToPAS perpetual calendar, and which, given a year, determines the day of the month of May on which this contest has place.

The data consists of a line with only the integer greater than 2010 and less than 3000, which identifies the year.

The result is also made up of a single line with an integer between 1 and 31 (inclusive), and that is the day on which the contest takes place in the year given away.

Example 1

Input 


2012

Output 


11

Example 2

Input 


2022

Output 


6
