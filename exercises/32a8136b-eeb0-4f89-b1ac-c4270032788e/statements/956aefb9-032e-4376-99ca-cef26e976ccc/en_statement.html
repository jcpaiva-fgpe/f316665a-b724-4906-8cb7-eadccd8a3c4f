Strong authentication

Congratulations! You have been admitted to the GIS (Security Interest Group). Your first task is to implement the strong authentication system required by the authentication of our website. We don't use keywords. To authenticate you, you will be presented with a challenge that will have to be replied in 10 milliseconds, without fail and at the first attempt. This means that the challenge will have to be answered by a program that will have to install on your authentication client. This program receives a string as a challenge and responds with another string of characters.
