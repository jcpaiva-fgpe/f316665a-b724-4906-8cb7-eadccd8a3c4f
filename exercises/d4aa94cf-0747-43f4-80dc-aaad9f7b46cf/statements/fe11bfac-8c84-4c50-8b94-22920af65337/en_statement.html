Problem F
Spiral of numbers

Problem

In this problem you must write a program that builds a spiral of numbers. The input consists of an integer N multiple of 4 or multiple of 4 plus 1 and N 100. Each turn of the spiral involves four numbers, except the innermost lap which may involve only a number. The spiral starts at number 1 and ends at number N . Each number occupies exactly the space of two houses (examples: '1', '10') and each line must be exactly 2 * (number of lines) characters. Here are some examples of spirals: 

For N = 8: For N = 13:
 1 2 1 2
   5 6 5 6
   8 7 9 10
 4 3 13
                            12 11
                           8 7
                         4 3

Input

A positive integer less than 100 and a multiple of 4 or a multiple of 4 plus 1, indicating the largest number of the spiral.

Output

Your program should write the spiral as in the examples above with the careful to represent each number with two spaces and place exactly the necessary spaces at the end of each line.

Input example 

16

Example output 

 1 2
   5 6
     9 10
      1314
      1615
    12 11
   8 7
 4 3
