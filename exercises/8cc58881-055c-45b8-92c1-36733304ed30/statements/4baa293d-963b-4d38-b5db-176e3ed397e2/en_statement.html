Ali Baba and Biometrics


The voice acts as a fingerprint to identify each individual, like the iris or its DNA. Its features are determined either by the way the vocal cords vibrate or by by patterns resulting from other physical aspects of the individual. Like would be the story of Ali Baba if the treasure cave was protected by a biometric system based on voice recognition? It would not be enough pronounce the magic formula "Open yourself Sesame". But, he would be able to take some advantage of the fact that the authentication system uses measures probabilistic and, consequently, being able to make wrong decisions (the false positives that would serve your interests)?

Task

The intention is to analyze a sequence of interactions between Ali Baba and biometric system of the cave and write a program to determine the maximum number of times in a row that managed to open the door without difficulties. The program will analyze a record of occurrences in which 1 1 designates opening without problems and 1 0 a failure . After a failure there will always be a sequence of zero or more failed attempts (each represented by 0 ) which will end with 1 (success) or 2 (withdrawal). Each input line has one occurrence, with the exception of of the last line which is always -1 -1 . The output will be a line with the requested amount.
