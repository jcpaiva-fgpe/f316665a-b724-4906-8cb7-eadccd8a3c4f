Emergency Phones

Problem

A given motorway concessionaire intends to install a emergency telephone network. However, due to the exorbitant price of electrical cables in recent times, the concessionaire is trying to solve the problem of sending electricity to phones in a way that does not force it to lay cables along the entire motorway. Although widely adopted on other motorways, in this area the panels solar panels are not an option as it is permanently bad weather.

However, all is not lost. Recently, a new wireless electricity transmission technology that, using very sophisticated directional antennas allow the direct sending of all the signal to a given phone whenever it initiates a call. With this electricity transmission technology, the electrical power only one unit decreases for every kilometer the signal travels.

Despite being enthusiastic about the idea, those responsible, zealous for For security reasons, they are intended to confirm that all emergency will have enough electricity to operate.

Assignment

Write a program that given the positions of the antennas and the phones and its powers (of emission of the antennas and of operation of the phones), check whether the phones can work or not. a phone simultaneously receive signals from several antennas, the power being total received equal to the sum of the powers received from each antenna. Assume that no more than one phone will be used at the same time.

Input

Corresponds to an installation plan of antennas and location of emergency phones. In the first line there is a integer which indicates the extension in Km (measured in a straight line) of the region in which the antennas will be installed, . In the following lines have the value of () and then pairs of lines: the first line of each pair has the position of the antenna () and the second its power (), measured in a unit specially developed for these antennas. Then you will find the phone number () and pairs of lines: a the first line of each pair has the position for each phone () and the second, the power needed for your operation ().

It consists of lines. A - th line indicates whether the - th emergency phone is functional or not: it will have YES if you stay and NO otherwise. They all end with line change.
