Crazy race

Problem

In an athletics event, not yet introduced in the world competitions for lack of consensus, the classification of each team is given by the number of elements that reached the goal and the time of arrival of the last. It is used a tiebreaker when athletes from different teams cross the goal at the same time and the teams they belong to have also finished the race with the same number of athletes. If the recorded image does not allow you to see which athlete stretched his neck the most, the total weight of the elements of the team is used to untie them.

Each team consists of three athletes. It is possible that some athletes give up. The teams in which they are ranked are ranked in the top places. more athletes managed to finish the race. A team is not classified if none of your athletes cross the goal.

Assignment

Writing a program that gives a sequence of arrivals determines the order classification of teams (in which at least one athlete has finished the proof).

Input

The first row has the number of teams registered for the race. At remaining lines contain the sequence of arrival of the athletes (already ties). Each value identifies only the number of the team to which belongs to the athlete who arrived in the corresponding position. Data ends with -1.

Teams are identified by consecutive integers greater than or equal a 1. There are no more than 200 teams.

Output

As many lines as the number of teams ranked: the first line indicates the number of the team that came first, the second will be the classified second and so on.
