Preciousness

Two friends who haven't seen each other for a few months have met twice on the same day at 7:58 and 14:05. One of them exclaimed, `` Only 367 passed minutes! '' and the other replied, `` You mean, 6 hours and 7 minutes ?! ''. But, if they had met again at 7:59, the first one would say `` Passed just 1 minute!''. The second would say `` In fact! ''. I would always say that if the interval was less than an hour. If they had met again at 13:58, the second would say `` You mean, 6 hours ?! '' and if it was at 8:59 already it would be `` You mean, 1 hour and 1 minute ?! ''. We intend to write dialogues identical to these.

Task

Write a program that produces the two sentences of the dialogue for two (consecutive) meetings, registered at hours and minutes and hours and minutes, being integers such that e , for . The values ​​of e are given on a line separated by a space. Each of the two sentences of the dialogue is written on a different line, ending with line change.

Example 1

Input 


7 58
14 5

Output 


Only 367 minutes have passed!
You mean, 6 hours and 7 minutes ?!

Example 2

Input 


2 1
2 48

Output 


Only 47 minutes have passed!
In fact!

Example 3

Input 


8 0
9 1

Output 


Only 61 minutes have passed!
You mean, 1 hour and 1 minute ?!
