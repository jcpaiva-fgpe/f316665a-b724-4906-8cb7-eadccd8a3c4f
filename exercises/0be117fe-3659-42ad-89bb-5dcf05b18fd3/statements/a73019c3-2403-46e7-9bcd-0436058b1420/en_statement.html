Genetics

The computer increasingly substitutes the microscope as an instrument fundamental research in Biology, having already given rise to an area of scientific research - Bioinformatics. As more and more information biological, especially genetic, is digitalized and available in databases data, it becomes possible to perform a certain type of analysis only with the computer. One of these analyzes is the count of occurrences of sequences of characters. In genetic research, strings are consisting only of the capitals A, G, C and T, corresponding to the bases of the DNA molecule: Adenine, Guanine, Cytosine and Thymine.

Task

Given two strings p and q , only with the uppercase A , G , C and T , we intend to count the number of occurrences of p in q . The two sequences of characters are given one on each line. The first line contains the sequence p to search and the second the q sequence. The length of p is a minimum of two characters and a maximum length of q , which does not exceed 100.

Example 1

Input 

AGCT
AGCTAGCT

Output 

two

Example 2

Input 

AAA
AAAAAAAAAA

Output 

8

Example 3

Input 

AGC
TTTTGT

Output 

0
