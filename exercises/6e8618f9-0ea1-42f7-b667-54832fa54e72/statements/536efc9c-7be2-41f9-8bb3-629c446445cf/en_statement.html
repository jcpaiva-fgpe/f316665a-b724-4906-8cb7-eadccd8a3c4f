Game of Secrets

Problem

In the interval of classes some students are playing the Secrets, grouped in circles with at least three students. A certain element in a circle says a secret to your left neighbor. This transmits it to the next and so successively until the secret arrives first, sometimes truly distorted! The greater the number of elements in the circle, the distorted comes the secret.

Assignment

Write a program to determine the size of the largest circle in a scenario involving students, numbered from 1 to , possibly grouped in one or more circles (of varying sizes). A sequence is given where is the student who was left of student 1, is the student who was to the left of student 2, and so on.

Input

In the first line there is the number of students (it is known that ). In the following lines there is the sequence that sets the stage.

Output

Will write the largest number of students in a circle, followed by a change of line.

3
two

1

3
